Sueca Card Game

About

This was a project for the Programming Lab II class, back in early 2013, during my bachelors in Computer Science. My group and I had to develop the Sueca Card Game in 3 different platforms while adopting MVC. The platforms included Windows Forms, Windows Phone and OpenSim.

Extra requirements

In addition to the game rules, we also had to implement some other basic functionalities, including shuffling, dealing, pontuation and history.
